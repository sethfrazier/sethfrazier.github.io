About Me

I wouldn't call myself a natural cartographer. I came into the world of cartography by way of GIS. My love of maps has slowly grown, thanks in large part to my graduate studies.
I work at a mining and exploration company here in the Northwestern United States, making all sorts of maps from the detailed to the general. My wife and I are parents to one very sassy little girl.
